A port of a screensaver i made for windows/android to GD directly based on MindCap's megacollab "ORBIT" (especially the ball pit)

- Customizable settings (timeout/speed/cube chance/orb count...)
- Uses your own cube
- Custom background color/opacity
- BLUR mode! (suggested mod)
